Information

Enigma is a six-chaptered Minecraft adventure, designed to deliver an immersive and story-driven experience while staying as close to vanilla Minecraft as possible. That means no mods, no datapacks, and no custom texturesâ€”just pure Minecraft creativity combined with custom mechanics through plugins.

Each chapter features a unique theme, custom structures, handcrafted dungeons, bosses, and deep lore. Players will explore mysterious landscapes, uncover ancient secrets, and face challenges that test their skill and knowledge. Players can upgrade their gear, acquire unique items, and join up to 20 other players in battle against the structures enemies!
